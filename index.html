Distribution_Agreement.pdf

Distribution Contract • 12 pages

Executive Summary

This distribution agreement contains several standard clauses but has potential issues in the royalty calculation section and lacks sufficient termination provisions.

Medium Risk Level

Key Findings

Royalty Calculation (Section 4.2)

The royalty calculation lacks clarity on digital streaming revenue. This could lead to disputes over payment amounts.

Termination Provisions (Section 8)

The termination clause is one-sided, favoring the distributor. Consider negotiating more balanced terms.

Territory Rights (Section 2.1)

The territorial rights are clearly defined and align with industry standards.

Missing Clauses

  • Audit Rights: No provision for the artist to audit the distributor's accounting records.
  • Reversion of Rights: No clause specifying when rights revert back to the artist.

Recommendations

  1. Revise Section 4.2 to clearly define royalty calculations for all distribution channels, including streaming platforms.
  2. Negotiate more balanced termination provisions in Section 8, including reciprocal rights.
  3. Add an audit rights clause allowing you to verify accounting records at least once per year.
  4. Include a reversion of rights clause that specifies when rights return to you if certain conditions are met.
